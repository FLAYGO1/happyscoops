Our Story

Born on Campus, Built with Purpose

I'm Uzoma — an IT student at Babcock University and the founder of Happy Scoops. I started this because I noticed something simple: students here had no real option for a quick, healthy, and genuinely satisfying snack.

So I started making parfaits. Not from a packet — from scratch. The yoghurt is made by me, using fresh, locally sourced ingredients. Every cup is assembled by hand, with fruit, granola, and toppings you actually choose yourself.

Happy Scoops isn't just a dessert. It's a nutritional snack that tastes like a treat — and it was made with Babcock students in mind.

Uzoma Dinobi Harry, Founder
